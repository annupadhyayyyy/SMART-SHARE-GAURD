🛑 Smart Share Guard
Chrome Extension to Reduce Online Misinformation
<BR>
🚀 Overview <BR>
Smart Share Guard is a Chrome extension designed to reduce the spread of misinformation by intervening at the moment of sharing. It introduces a pause, displays credibility warnings, and encourages users to verify content before sharing. Instead of blocking content, it promotes responsible and informed decision-making. <BR>
🎯 Problem<BR>
Misinformation spreads quickly because users often share content impulsively without verifying its authenticity. Emotional headlines and unverified claims further accelerate its reach. <BR>
💡 Solution <BR>
Smart Share Guard adds a friction layer during the sharing process: <BR>
Detects share intent <BR>
Introduces a delay <BR>
Displays warning messages <BR>
Provides fact-check guidance <BR>
⚙️ How It Works <BR>
User clicks Share <BR>
⏳ Delay screen appears <BR>
⚠️ Warning popup shows trust score <BR>
🔎 User can check facts <BR>
✅ Final confirmation encourages responsible sharing <BR>
