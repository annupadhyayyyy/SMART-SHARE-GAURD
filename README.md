🛑 Smart Share Guard
Chrome Extension to Reduce Online Misinformation
🚀 Overview
Smart Share Guard is a Chrome extension designed to reduce the spread of misinformation by intervening at the moment of sharing. It introduces a pause, displays credibility warnings, and encourages users to verify content before sharing. Instead of blocking content, it promotes responsible and informed decision-making.
🎯 Problem
Misinformation spreads quickly because users often share content impulsively without verifying its authenticity. Emotional headlines and unverified claims further accelerate its reach.
💡 Solution
Smart Share Guard adds a friction layer during the sharing process:
Detects share intent
Introduces a delay
Displays warning messages
Provides fact-check guidance
⚙️ How It Works
User clicks Share
⏳ Delay screen appears
⚠️ Warning popup shows trust score
🔎 User can check facts
✅ Final confirmation encourages responsible sharing
